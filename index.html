Ti Wang

I am a first-year PhD student at Mathis Laboratory of Adaptive Intelligence at EPFL, under the supervision of Prof. Mackenzie Mathis.

I obtained my Master's degree from Peking University, China, in July 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Hong Liu and Prof. Mengyuan Liu. Additionally, I was a visiting student at the Multimedia and Human Understanding Group (MHUG), University of Trento, Italy, under the supervision of Prof. Nicu Sebe. Previously, I also worked closely with Xia Li (now at ETH Zurich), Wenhao Li (now at NTU), and Yingxuan You (now at EPFL).

My current research interest focus on 2D/3D pose and shape estimation in images and videos.
