Ti Wang (王体)

I am an incoming PhD student at the Mathis Laboratory of Adaptive Intelligence at EPFL, underline the supervision of Prof. Mackenzie Mathis.

I will obtain my Master's degree from Peking University, China, in July 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Hong Liu and Prof. Mengyuan Liu. Additionally, I was a visiting student at the Multimedia and Human Understanding Group (MHUG), University of Trento, Italy, under the supervision of Prof. Nicu Sebe. I am also working closely with Xia Li from ETH Zurich.

My current research interest focus on 3D human pose and shape estimation in images/videos using deep learning and optimization approaches.
